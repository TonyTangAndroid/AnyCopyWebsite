FAQ

  • Question 1: why should I use AnyCopy？


    When you copy notes from SMS, twitter, Facebook in mobile device, chances are that you would like to save it. But you have to go through several steps to do it. AnyCopy will automatically save the notes copied to Clipboard. It does not matter which app that you are using, as long as you like the text displayed in the screen, all you have to do is to copy it and it will be saved and synced to Cloud by AnyCopy to make sure that you never miss any single word that matters to you.

  • Question 2:Is there any other outstanding features?


    AnyCopy saves time in your daily usage of inputting your email address or timestamp. Whenever you are using your email address to register an account, are you tired of inputting the same email address again and again? AnyCopy provide Quick Launch and Copy action, which helps you input your email address in 3 three seconds.

  • Question 3: should I have concerns if I save password in AnyCopy?



    We spare no efforts in enhancing the security. Currently your data is protected by four security layers:

    1，App Passcode. Users could set an App Passcode to lock the app in certain time.
    2，The Key to a folder. Users could set a key to the folder to prevent being accessed by others.
    3，Encrypt all data in cache, even your device is lost, the data is still secure.
    4，Remotely remove devices data. If your device is lost, you could easily erase the data in the lost device.
    5，All your notes will be encrypted in Server.

  • Question 4：could it be used in offline mode？


    AnyCopy fully supports in offline mode. Even there is no network, AnyCopy could still save the notes copied in Clipboard. And the notes will be automatically synced to Cloud on the network is connected.
